What's Your Show Count?

v 0.0.1

In early 2013, the Umphrey's community was introduced to the concept of a streaming show count and how it factored in to their existing number of shows seen. Fans new and old chimed in on this phenomenon, with many skeptics questioning the legitmacy of what became known as an adjusted show count. Even Joel had something to say:

.@shimmerinshell great question. Anyone else want to weigh in? Do pantsless couch tour shows go to show count? Or, new category?

— Joel Cummins (@goldlikejoel) January 22, 2013

Over the past two years, a small group of dedicated fans, known as the Adjusted Show Count Collective, or ASCC, have been working on an algorithm that considers a variety of criteria to determine one's true show count. While the formula is still a work in progress, the alpha status algorithm currently uses states, days of the week, and streamed shows as the primary multipliers for the adjusted count.

We are working with the creators of All Things Umphrey's to allow remote authentication through this site so we can scrape the data directly from your ATN account. In the mean time, you will have to manually input your stats. Check out the links below for more information.
